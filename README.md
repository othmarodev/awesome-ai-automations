> A curated list of automations, bots, and GitHub Actions — many powered by LLMs — that do the boring work for you. Cron jobs, CI pipelines, and assistants for developers, teams, and small businesses.

## Developers — high leverage

### Social & content

- [Typefully](https://typefully.com) - Turn one long piece into threads and scheduled posts.
- [Postiz](https://github.com/gitroomhq/postiz-app) - Schedule and cross-post to every social network from one editor. 🔓
- [Hypefury](https://hypefury.com) - Recycle your best replies into standalone posts.
- [Satori](https://github.com/vercel/satori) - Generate social/carousel images from HTML & CSS. 🔓
- [F5Bot](https://f5bot.com) - Email alerts whenever your keywords appear on Reddit, Hacker News, or Lobsters.

### Developer life

- [Geekbot](https://geekbot.com) - Async standups and surveys that summarize team progress.
- [git-cliff](https://github.com/orhun/git-cliff) - Generate a changelog from your commit history. 🔓
- [GitHub Income Scout](https://github.com/George4177/github-income-scout) - Screens public issues into Markdown, JSON, and CSV reports. 🔓
- [ChatGPT-CodeReview](https://github.com/anc95/ChatGPT-CodeReview) - LLM that reviews your pull requests inline. 🔓
- [Renovate](https://github.com/renovatebot/renovate) - Automated dependency updates with grouped PRs. 🔓
- [Uptime Kuma](https://github.com/louislam/uptime-kuma) - Self-hosted uptime, domain expiry, and SSL certificate monitoring. 🔓
- [Caveman](https://github.com/JuliusBrussee/caveman) - Cut AI agent output ~65% by teaching Claude Code, Cursor, and 30+ agents to speak tersely without losing technical accuracy. Ships `/caveman`, `caveman-commit`, and `caveman-review`. By [Julius Brussee](https://github.com/JuliusBrussee). 🔓

### Everyday life

- [n8n](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n) - Fair-code workflow automation: connect apps, build cron jobs, add LLM steps. 🔓
- [n8n workflow templates](https://n8n.io/workflows) - Hundreds of copy-paste automation recipes.
- [Shortwave](https://shortwave.com) - AI inbox that categorizes, summarizes, and drafts replies.
- [Fireflies](https://fireflies.ai) - Records meetings and turns them into notes and action items.
- [Readwise](https://readwise.io) - Save links and articles, get spaced-repetition digests.

## Business & professions

### Small business

- [Landbot](https://landbot.io) - No-code WhatsApp/web chatbots for FAQs and bookings.
- [GoHighLevel](https://www.gohighlevel.com) - Instant lead response, follow-up, and CRM automation.
- [Chaser](https://www.chaserhq.com) - Automated invoice payment reminders and chasing.
- [Typebot](https://github.com/baptisteArno/typebot.io) - Build conversational order/booking bots. 🔓
- [Sortly](https://www.sortly.com) - Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts.

### Other professions

- [ChatPDF](https://www.chatpdf.com) - Ask questions and summarize long contracts and PDFs.
- [Expensify](https://www.expensify.com) - Snap a receipt, auto-extract and categorize expenses.
- [PandaDoc](https://www.pandadoc.com) - Generate quotes and proposals as PDFs from input.
- [Calendly](https://calendly.com) - Scheduling with automated appointment reminders.

### Personal & family

- [Mealime](https://www.mealime.com) - Weekly meal plans that generate a grocery list.
- [Dex](https://getdex.com) - Personal CRM that reminds you to follow up with contacts.
- [TripIt](https://www.tripit.com) - Parses flight/hotel confirmations into one itinerary.
- [Rocket Money](https://www.rocketmoney.com) - Tracks subscriptions and flags price hikes to cancel.
- [Medisafe](https://www.medisafe.com) - Medication and appointment reminders with caregiver alerts.

### Freelancers & creators

- [Teal](https://www.tealhq.com) - Tailors your resume per job posting and tracks applications.
- [Opus Clip](https://www.opus.pro) - Turns one long video into short clips for every platform.
- [Zapier](https://zapier.com) - Trigger actions across 6,000+ apps from a form or event.

## Developers — deep (GitHub Action + LLM)

### Tests, quality & security

- [Qodo](https://www.qodo.ai) - Generates and maintains unit tests for changed files.
- [Codecov](https://about.codecov.io) - Coverage gates that block PRs which drop coverage.
- [Gitleaks](https://github.com/gitleaks/gitleaks) - Scans diffs for committed secrets before merge. 🔓

### Documentation

- [readme.so](https://readme.so) - Assemble a complete README from a repo.
- [Mintlify](https://mintlify.com) - Docs that flag drift when code changes.
- [Scalar](https://github.com/scalar/scalar) - Auto-generate browsable API docs from OpenAPI. 🔓

### Pull requests & repo hygiene

- [CodeRabbit](https://coderabbit.ai) - Writes PR titles, summaries, and review comments from the diff.
- [actions/labeler](https://github.com/actions/labeler) - Auto-label PRs based on changed files. 🔓
- [pr-size-labeler](https://github.com/CodelyTV/pr-size-labeler) - Warns when a PR is too big and suggests splitting it. 🔓
- [actions/stale](https://github.com/actions/stale) - Nudges and archives stale issues and PRs. 🔓
- [all-contributors](https://github.com/all-contributors/all-contributors) - Welcomes and credits first-time contributors. 🔓

### Code, releases & deploy

- [Hygen](https://github.com/jondot/hygen) - Scaffold consistent boilerplate from templates. 🔓
- [git-auto-commit-action](https://github.com/stefanzweifel/git-auto-commit-action) - Format/fix and auto-commit back to the PR. 🔓
- [semantic-release](https://github.com/semantic-release/semantic-release) - Automate versioning, tagging, publishing, and release notes. 🔓
- [Sentry](https://github.com/getsentry/sentry) - Turns runtime errors into deduplicated issues with suggested fixes. 🔓
